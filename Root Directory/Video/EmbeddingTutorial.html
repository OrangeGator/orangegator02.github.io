How to embed videos from Youtube

Step 1

When you embed videos from youtube, you need to use the iframe tag.

Step 2

Below the video you want to embed, you will find "copy" which is in yellow. Click there.

Step 3

You will fing "embed" which is in yellow. Click there.

Step 4

You will see the code of the video. Click "copy" in yellow in the image.

Step 5

Paste the code inside the iframe tag.

Step 6

The video will appear on you website.
